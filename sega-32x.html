Sega 32X flashcarts do not exist, presently. However, if you do own a 32X Add-on in addition to your Mega Drive or Genesis, the following flashcarts will allow 32X passthrough (i.e., they work):

Status Make Brand Model Notes MSRP
Shipping Krikzz Mega Everdrive Pro (Also plays Mega-CD/Sega CD as well as MSU’s.) ~USD$199
Shipping Krikzz Mega Everdrive X5   ~USD$55
Shipping Krikzz Mega Everdrive X3   ~USD$39
Legacy Krikzz Mega Everdrive X7   —
Legacy Krikzz Everdrive MD v3   —
Legacy Krikzz Everdrive MD v2   —
Legacy Krikzz Mega Everdrive v1   —

Note: none of these, save the Pro support the Mega-CD/Sega CD. Despite this single model, none of the current or past models support the six (6) 32X-CD titles.
